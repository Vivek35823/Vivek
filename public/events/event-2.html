About This Workshop

Join us for an inspiring and transformative Youth Leadership Workshop where young minds come together to explore the essence of true leadership through the teachings of Swami Vivekananda. This interactive workshop is designed to help you develop essential leadership skills, build confidence, and learn how to inspire and serve others.

During this workshop, we will delve into practical leadership principles that empower you to make a positive impact in your communities. Through engaging discussions, real-world case studies, and interactive exercises, you will discover your unique leadership potential and develop strategies to apply these lessons in your daily life.

Key Focus: This workshop emphasizes the development of character, courage, and compassion—the three pillars of effective leadership according to Swami Vivekananda's philosophy.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can attend this workshop?

This workshop is designed for young people aged 15-35 who are interested in developing their leadership skills and exploring Swami Vivekananda's teachings.

Is this workshop free?

Yes! This is a free workshop organized by VIVEK as part of our mission to develop conscious youth.

Can I attend in-person?

Yes, we offer both online and in-person options. Limited seats available for in-person participation.

Will I receive a certificate?

Absolutely! All participants will receive a digital certificate of participation after the workshop.
