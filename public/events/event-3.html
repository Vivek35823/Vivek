About This Study Circle

Welcome to our Monthly Study Circle—a dedicated space for seekers, scholars, and lovers of Swami Vivekananda's philosophy to come together and explore his complete works in depth. This is more than just a reading group; it's a community of minds committed to understanding and applying Vivekananda's teachings in our modern lives.

Each month, we dive deep into specific themes, texts, and teachings from Swami Vivekananda's vast body of work, including his speeches, letters, essays, and conversations. Through collaborative discussion and scholarly exploration, participants gain new insights and practical wisdom that can transform their understanding of life, spirituality, and service.

This Month's Focus: We will be exploring "Karma and Duty" from Vivekananda's teachings and their application in modern life and decision-making.
